Common questions.

Everything you need to know about your personal social media platform, from ownership and privacy to analytics and visitor interactions.

Who owns and controls the platform?

You have complete ownership and control. This is your personal social media platform where you decide what to share, who can interact, and how your content is presented. You're the admin, curator, and community manager all in one.

What privacy settings are available?

You control visibility settings for your posts and profile. While the platform is designed for sharing, you can moderate comments, block users, and choose which analytics data to display publicly. Your personal information remains private unless you choose to share it.

What do the analytics include?

Built-in analytics track daily and monthly visitor statistics, including unique visitors, page views, engagement metrics (likes, comments), and peak activity times. You'll get insights into your audience's behavior and content performance through an intuitive dashboard.

How long does setup take?

Typical setup takes 1-2 weeks from initial consultation to launch. This includes platform customization, content migration (if needed), analytics integration, and testing. We'll work with you to ensure everything is configured to your preferences before going live.

How do visitors interact with my content?

Visitors can like your posts, leave comments, and share content (if you enable sharing). All interactions are visible to you in real-time through your dashboard. You can respond to comments, moderate discussions, and build a community around your shared experiences.
